HOW TO PLAY
REALM OF REGIONS

Goal

Defeat every region boss to unlock the next region. Clear all 4 regions to win!

World Map

Pick a region, fight enemies to earn gold and XP, then challenge the boss.

Battle

Attack - Physical damage.
Magic - Spend 5 MP for a magic blast.
Defend - Block next hit, reflect 10 damage back. (10 uses max)
Item - Use a potion or food from your bag.
Act - Enemy misses their turn and becomes spareable.
Spare - End the fight peacefully after Act. Bosses will punish you for this!

Secret Bosses

Defeat 3 enemies in a row in any region to unlock a secret boss!

Shop and Inn

Buy weapons and potions. Rest at the inn for 15g to restore HP and MP.

Leveling Up

Earn XP from battles to level up and increase all stats!
HOW TO PLAY
REALM OF REGIONS 2

Goal

Defeat all 5 demon bosses across 5 regions to conquer the realm!

World Map

Fight enemies to earn gold and XP. Defeat the demon boss to unlock the next region and claim their Heart!

Battle

Attack - Physical strike.
Magic - 5 MP for a magic blast.
Defend - Block next hit, reflect 10 damage.
Item - Use potions or throw a bomb.
Act - Enemy misses turn, becomes spareable.
Spare - Spare regular enemies. Bosses retaliate instantly!
Heart - Use a Heart power earned from defeating a demon boss.

Heart Powers

Wild Growth restores HP and MP fully. Rockslide and Inferno Blast deal heavy damage. Absolute Zero freezes the enemy. Shadow Drain steals HP from them.

Bestiary

Every enemy you defeat gets logged. Try to collect them all!

Leveling Up

Each level up fully restores your HP and MP!
HOW TO PLAY
REALM OF REGIONS 3

Goal

Defeat all 6 Titans across 6 regions. Beat the Desert Titan to win and earn Game Points!

World Map

Regions unlock in order. Defeat 5 enemies in a region to reveal a secret Knight. Defeat the Titan to advance.

Battle

Attack - Double damage against Titans!
Magic - Spend MP to cast spells. More unlock as your kill count grows.
Hearts - Special powers dropped by enemies.
Tattle - Reveal enemy stats in the Tattle Book.
Defend - Block and reflect 10 damage.
Act - Enemy misses turn, becomes spareable.
Spare - Spare regular enemies. Titans destroy you for trying!

Hearts

Wild Growth heals all HP and restores all MP. Rockslide and Inferno Blast deal 35 damage. Absolute Zero freezes the enemy. Shadow Drain steals 20 HP. Desert Dash dodges 3 attacks.

Spells

Start with 5 spells. Unlock more every 10 kills up to 100. Realm Break is the strongest!

2 Player Mode

Players take turns. If one falls the other keeps fighting!

Game Points

Beat the game by defeating the Desert Titan to earn 10 GP. Spend GP in the hub to unlock Mini Games!
