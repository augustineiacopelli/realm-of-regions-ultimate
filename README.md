# Realm of Regions Ultimate

**Designed and created by Simon Iacopelli**

A complete RPG collection featuring all three Realm of Regions games plus a full mini game arcade — all in a single file.

---

## What's Inside

### The Three Games

**Realm of Regions** — The original. Four regions: Forest, Mountain, Lava, and Frost. Fight enemies, defeat demon bosses, unlock secret bosses, and conquer the realm.

**Realm of Regions 2** — Five regions including the Shadow Region. Collect Heart powers from defeated demons and use them in battle. Features a Bestiary to track every enemy you defeat.

**Realm of Regions 3: The Desert Awakens** — Six regions including the Desert. Features the Tattle Book, a full spell system that unlocks as your kill count grows, secret Knights, and 2-Player mode. Beating the Desert Titan earns Game Points.

### Game Points and Mini Games

Earn **GP (Game Points)** by playing — 1 GP per minute in Realm of Regions games, 2 GP per minute in mini games. Beating Realm of Regions 3 awards a bonus 10 GP. GP is capped at 100.

Spend **10 GP** to unlock a randomly selected mini game from a pool of 100 slots.

**Mini Games included so far:**
- Speed Racer *(free starter)* — Steer left and right to avoid obstacles and reach 500m
- Math Blaster — Solve 10 math problems against the clock
- Flip Match — Classic memory card matching game
- Number Guesser — Guess the secret number with higher/lower hints
- Card Clash Mini — Draw cards against the CPU, higher card wins

**Play limits:** Realm of Regions games have a 20-minute session limit with a 5-minute break (spend 10 GP to extend by 10 minutes). Mini games have a 30-minute session limit with a 10-minute break.
